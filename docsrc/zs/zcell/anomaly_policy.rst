anomaly_policy
==============

The following methods allow for interaction with the ZCell Anomaly Policy API endpoints.
Includes listing, creating, updating, deleting anomaly policies, toggling their status,
and retrieving policy logs and violations.

Methods are accessible via ``zcell.anomaly_policy``
